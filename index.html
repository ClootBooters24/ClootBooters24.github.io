Brain Ballast Sensor

**Problem:** Deterioration of railroad ballast due to water pressure buildup can lead to instability and derailments.

**Goal:** Develop a wireless sensor system to accurately measure localized pore water pressure within ballast and transmit data for real-time monitoring.

**Key Features:**

  • Wireless Technology: Utilizes Bluetooth for data transmission.
  • Sensor Integration: Combines a pressure sensor with an accelerometer for additional data.
  • Customizable Housing: Designed to fit within ballast particles.
  • Android App: Provides a user-friendly interface for data collection and analysis.
